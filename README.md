ProvidAI
Bridging the gap between students and their rightful scholarships.

Millions of dollars in scholarship funds go unclaimed every year. For a regular student, finding a scholarship is like looking for a needle in a haystack of fragmented websites, dead links, and complex eligibility criteria. The core issue is navigation students don't know what they are eligible for or how to get there.

ProvidAI is a smart navigation engine that acts as a digital compass for students. By entering a single profile, the system instantly filters through thousands of opportunities and provides:
1. Instant Eligibility Matching:Shows exactly which scholarships you qualify for.
2. The Roadmap:A clear, step-by-step guide on how to complete the application for each scheme.

🚀 Key Features
One-Time Profiler:Enter your academic and socio-economic details once (GPA, Income, Category).
Intelligent Filtering:No more manual searching. Our engine matches your data against complex rule sets.
Step-by-Step Navigation:** Provides a personalized "To-Do" checklist for every scholarship, including required documents.
Direct Portal Integration:One-click redirection to official application pages (SSP, NSP, etc.).
* **Benefit Transparency:** Displays the potential financial aid amount clearly for every match.
